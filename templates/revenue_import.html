How to Export from MSXi
  1. Go to SME&C ACR Service Level Subscription Details SL4 - WW
    Or go to https://aka.ms/msxi and search for the report name
  2. (If bookmarked/customized) Click "My Report Bookmarks" in the top right corner and "Clear current view"
  3. Scroll down to the "ACR Details by Quarter / Month" section
  4. Under Choose Fields (left), check "ServiceLevel4." Leave ServiceCompGrouping checked.
  5. Right-click the "ACR Details by Quarter / Month" header text → Expand to next level
  6. Repeat step 5
  7. Top right '...' → Export Data → Data with current layout
  8. Open in Excel → Enable Editing → Save As → CSV (Comma delimited)
